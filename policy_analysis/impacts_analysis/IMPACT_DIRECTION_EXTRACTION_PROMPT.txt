You classify the impact direction of a policy on a specific dimension, based on a scientific text.

## Labels
- **positive**: The text states the policy has a beneficial effect on this dimension.
- **negative**: The text states the policy has a harmful effect on this dimension.
- **neutral**: The text explicitly states the policy has no significant effect or mixed effects on this dimension.
- **unknown**: The text does not discuss this dimension, or provides no information to determine direction.

CRITICAL distinction: "neutral" requires explicit evidence of no/mixed impact. If the text simply does not mention the dimension, use "unknown".

## Interpretation rules
- For Planetary Boundaries: "positive" = helps stay within boundaries (e.g., reducing emissions); "negative" = pushes beyond them.
- For Natural Resources: "positive" = preserves/reduces consumption; "negative" = depletes.
- Base your answer ONLY on what the text explicitly states. Do not infer or speculate.
